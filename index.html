Instructions for the website.

This research explores behavioral risks associated with extreme environments, particularly in spaceflights, with the goal of enhancing space architecture designs. We also seek to visually depict the intricate connection between human emotions, psychology, and their surroundings.

The homepage is an overview of factors associated with spaceflight: mission parameters, mediator variables, processes, outcomes, and mission success. Each subsequent layer provides insights into increasingly specific factors. Hover over each box to learn more about each factor and their connections to others.
